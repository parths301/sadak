Risk Profile Assessment

No investment is without risk. The aggressive portfolio intentionally embraces higher risk for higher potential returns. Understanding this profile is critical. The primary risk is market volatility, which is mitigated by a long-term horizon and diversification.

Market Risk (High): The portfolio's value will fluctuate with the stock market. A long-term view (10+ years) is the best mitigation.

Credit/Default Risk (High): Concentrated in the P2P lending portion. Mitigation requires extreme diversification across many borrowers.

Liquidity Risk (Medium): Funds are locked in for ELSS (3 years) and SGBs (8 years). This portfolio is not for short-term needs.

Concentration Risk (Low): While individual stocks are held, the Core-Satellite structure and diversified funds keep this risk managed.

The Journey Ahead: Building Wealth

Investing is not a one-time event; it's a lifelong journey. After this initial investment, the most powerful step is to cultivate a habit of regular saving through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP).

Step 1: Invest

Deploy the ₹70,000 as per the allocation plan. Get your capital working in the market.

Step 2: Monitor & Rebalance

Review your portfolio annually. Sell some winners and buy some laggards to return to your target allocation. This enforces "buy low, sell high."

Step 3: Compound with SIPs

Start a monthly SIP into your core funds. This consistency is the true engine of long-term wealth creation, leveraging compounding and rupee cost averaging.
